YONGJUN LEE

3D DESIGNER

BIOGRAPHY

Yongjun Lee (*1999, South Korea) is a designer living and working in Germany. Lee combines interdisciplinary studies with product design and experimental media design.
Lee's most recent works include the 2023 graduation projects "Virtual-Production, Real-Time-Compositing Exhibition" (2023), "The Gwanghwa: A Film Made with Unreal Engine" (2023), and "Traverse: Application Design" (2023).

Yongjun Lee received a bachelor's degree in Digital Contents Design Intensive Track from Hankyong National University in February 2024.
