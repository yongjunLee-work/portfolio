YONGJUN LEE

3D DESIGNER

Ddingdong / 2022

Last-Mile Delivery Robot / Perovskite-Solar-Cell / HKNU-Design The Greatest Design Award 2022

Ddingdong aims to solve the 'delivery cost burden' for caterers and consumers by using a perovskite solar cell delivery robot to enhance sustainability and reduce labor costs.
As an 'autonomous driving food delivery robot' that utilizes pedestrian roads and less crowded back roads in complex urban areas, it offers Last Mile Food Delivery Robotics as a Service (RaaS).

During my junior year at university, I spent a semester deeply engaged in studying Service Design and Experience Design. This coursework sparked my interest in user-centered design, as it emphasized not only functional aspects but also aesthetic elements, usability, and emotional satisfaction. In the second semester of my junior year, I participated in a multidisciplinary project on Perovskite Solar Cells, which involved collaboration between the Design, Business Administration, and Chemical Engineering departments. This project required an in-depth understanding of knowledge from both Business Administration and Chemical Engineering to achieve effective design outcomes. The collaborative efforts across these three disciplines culminated in a successful and innovative solution.
