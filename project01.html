THE GWANGHWA - UNREAL ENGINE SHORT FILM

FICTION / 2:05 MIN / UNREAL ENGINE5 / SHORTFILM / 4K / GRADUATION PROJECT 2023

North Korea's victory following the Korean War and its occupation of South Korea resulted in numerous changes by 2024.
The inability of our people to endure communist rule led to a renewed fight for South Korean independence.
The creation of a resistance-themed film showcases the intense battles to reclaim hope for South Korea.
